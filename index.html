What kind of game is Merge Fruit?

At its core, this is a matching game where players combine the same fruits to create new fruits of higher levels. The goal is to clear the fruit board by merging them until they reach the highest level, the Golden Watermelon. The gameplay is simple but engaging, as players must plan their moves strategically to maximize scores and clear the board effectively.

How to play Merge Fruit

Players start with a grid of fruits, and they must Merge Fruit to create new, more valuable fruits. To Merge Fruit, players simply swipe two identical fruits together. When two fruits are merged, they create a new fruit that is one level higher than the original two fruits. For example, combining two lemons will produce a coconut

There are over 200 different fruits to merge in Merge Fruit, and each fruit has its own value. The rarer the fruit, the more valuable it is. Players can earn coins by merging fruits, and they can use these coins to buy new fruits, power-ups, and other items. The ultimate goal of the game is to merge as many fruits as possible and reach the highest level.

Tips and Tricks for Success

While the gameplay of Merge Fruit may seem straightforward, there are some tips and tricks that can help players achieve higher scores and clear levels more efficiently. Here are a few strategies to keep in mind while playing:

  • Plan: Before making any moves, take a moment to survey the board and plan out your next few moves. This will help you make strategic merges and avoid getting stuck with no available moves.
  • Focus on creating high-value fruits: While it may be tempting to merge every fruit you see, try to focus on creating higher-value fruits. These will earn you more points and help you clear the board faster.
